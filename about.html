Our Story

From a Two-Person Lab to a Global Cyber Force

In 2008, Eade Marren and Savannah Nguyen left government intelligence with a clear mission: build a cybersecurity firm that treats clients as partners, not ticket numbers.

Starting in a San Francisco garage, they built STACKLY on three principles — deep technical expertise, proactive threat hunting, and radical transparency. Today, 120+ experts across 8 global offices carry that mission every day.
