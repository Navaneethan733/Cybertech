Methodology

A Proven 6-Phase Engagement Process

Every engagement follows our battle-tested methodology, ensuring thorough coverage with clear, actionable outcomes at every phase.

01
Discovery & Scoping

Map attack surface, understand business context, define engagement boundaries, rules of engagement, and success criteria.

02
Reconnaissance & Threat Modelling

OSINT gathering, passive footprinting, asset enumeration, and threat actor profiling relevant to your industry and stack.

03
Active Testing & Exploitation

Controlled, authorized attack simulations using industry tools and custom exploits developed by our in-house research team.

04
Risk Analysis & Prioritization

Every finding is rated by CVSS severity, business impact, and exploitability — giving clear remediation priorities.

05
Executive & Technical Reporting

Board-level executive summary plus detailed technical report with proof-of-concept evidence and reproduction steps.

06
Remediation Support & Re-test

Work alongside your dev teams during fixes, then provide a free re-test to validate all critical findings are fully resolved.
